Artificial Intelligence

The following is a brief overview of the power of Artificial Intelligence and Genetic Algorithms.

Artificial Intelligence is where computers mimic the way in which humans think, behave, or evolve in order to find a solution. They pretend to be humanlike - only they do it really fast. Some AI systems are called "Expert Systems" in that they are "hard-wired" to have all the information needed to build something like and F-18 fighter. IF anything is found that it doesn't know, a team of human experts get together and come up with an answer and then build it in to the "Expert System" so all Technicians and Engineers can rely on it. However, Expert Systems deal with known problems meaning they are not intended to learn or adapt.

Solution Hunter is based on a model called Genetic Algorithms, which - in its enhanced form - has proven to be very powerful and service a great many needs with less effort.

Hunter defines populations of individuals who have tools for solving a problem. Some are better than others and most of them will intermarry and give birth to children that are a blend of their parents' characteristics. Suffering mutations and plagues, they evolve quickly over thousands of generations and migrate toward a best solution in lightning speed. However, in this case, thousands of generations occur very quickly and are enhanced by simultaneous use of multiple computers.
