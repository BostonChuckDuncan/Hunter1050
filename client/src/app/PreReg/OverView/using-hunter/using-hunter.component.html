Using Hunter

Hunter works along side of the financial manager, engineer, or researcher.

It relies on the knowledge their human partner(s) have accrued from their knowledge and years of experience. Hunter then can wade through complex subjects consisting of huge possibilities to help bring focus to possible solutions.

In business and finance, it has been proven effective in investments and business strategy.

Hunter can sense changes in the problem environment and adapt as needed.

The application has documentation and tutorials to help get you started and models suited for your field of interest.
