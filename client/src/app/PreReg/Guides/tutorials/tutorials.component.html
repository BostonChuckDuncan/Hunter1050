Hunter Tutorials

Tutorials are being written (both written and online video) to guide new users through the steps of setting up basic and advanced applications.

Basic tutorials are fixed: in that they address all the general uses and featuers of Hunter

The Advanced tutorials are a part of the licensed version of the application as purchased by a specific company for their unique type and methods of research and focus on the custom Genetic-backend.

Note: These tutorials are geared toward the company's specific research area whether it be physics/materials, biomedical, weather, ....
